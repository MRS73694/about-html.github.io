The Text Style Tags

Tags Use
b Makes the enclosed text bold
i Makes the enclosed text italics
u Makes the enclosed text underlined
big Makes the enclosed text bigger in size than the surrounding text by 2 points
small Makes the enclosed text smaller by 2 points
sup Turns the enclosed text into a superscript
sub Turns the enclosed text into a subscript
