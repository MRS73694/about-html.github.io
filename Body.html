The Body Tag and its Attributes

Attribute Use
bgcolor Sets the background colour of the webpage
background Specifies the url/URL of the background image
text Specifies the text colour
topmargin Specifies the top margin of a webpage (i.e., the margin of the text from the top of a web page)
leftmargin Specifies the left margin of a webpage (i.e., the margin of the text from the left side of a web page)
